In tune with the future

We are a young distributing company. Our team is made of people that understand the target and being part of it is our main differentiator. We sell what we approve, what we like and what we love. We are indeed our first customers.

How to sell something we do not believe in?Our portfolio is exclusive and innovative. We bring worldwide trends to offer people unique products that they would like to buy, but didn’t find yet.

Enterprising with purpose

Kalimed was born looking to the future. More than a wholesale company focused on well-being and sustainability,we must make the difference.

We are in alignment with the growing trend of people’s interests in products made consciously for the next generations. Ecologic, organic, natural, biodegradable, fair-trade, cruelty-free, vegan and everything else.

Everyone doing its own part. And our mission is to gather companies and brands in a groundbreaking distribution. We are aware of our responsibilities, the next generations and the future. The world is changing, and we want to contribute actively for this constant transformation.
